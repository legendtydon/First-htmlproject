Pollination

Pollination is the first step of a seed making.

Pollinators such as Insects and Birds go from plant to plant to collect Nectar

As it is going from plant to plant, pollen(made by the Anther) stick to it, because it is moving from two or more plants, pollen on the pollinator sticks to the Stigma.

The stigma is part of the female fart of the plant.

Fertilization

When the pollen sticks to the stigma, it forms pollen tubes and goes throught the style and into the ovule which are located in the ovary.
